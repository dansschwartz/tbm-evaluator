# TBM Evaluator
### AI-Powered Player Evaluation for Youth Sports

---

## Stop using spreadsheets. Start making data-driven decisions.

TBM Evaluator is an AI-native player evaluation platform that helps coaches score players on their phones, generate instant reports, and send personalized report cards to parents — all in minutes, not days.

Built for youth sports organizations that want professional-grade evaluations without the professional-grade price tag.

---

## The Problem

Every tryout season, coaches face the same headaches:

📋 **Paper forms and spreadsheets** — hours of manual data entry after evaluations
⏰ **Delayed feedback** — parents wait weeks for results
🎲 **Subjective decisions** — without data, team placement feels like guessing
📊 **No historical tracking** — can't show a player's growth over time
💰 **Expensive alternatives** — SkillShark charges $5-$15 per player per year

---

## The Solution

### Score → Report → Send — in minutes, not days.

**1. Design Your Evaluation** (5 minutes)
Pick from pre-built sport templates or customize your own. Define skills, scoring scales, and weights.

**2. Score on Any Phone** (During the event)
Evaluators open the mobile app, enter their access code, and start scoring. Works offline too.

**3. AI Generates Reports** (Instant)
One click generates personalized report cards with AI-written narratives, strengths, improvements, and recommendations.

**4. Parents Get Results** (Same day)
Email report cards directly to parents. They see scores, rankings, visual charts, and coach recommendations.

**5. Draft Balanced Teams** (Minutes)
AI auto-balances teams based on scores. Or use manual draft mode with live rankings.

## Features

### 📱 Mobile Scoring
- Score players on any phone or tablet
- Touch-friendly sliders, toggles, and timers
- Built-in stopwatch for timed drills (40-yard dash, etc.)
- Add comments and notes per skill
- **Works offline** — scores sync when signal returns
- Multiple evaluators score simultaneously

### 🤖 AI-Powered Reports
- **Narrative summaries** — "Marcus shows exceptional ball control and game intelligence..."
- **Strengths & improvements** — auto-identified from score patterns
- **Development recommendations** — "Ready for Travel level" or "Recommend Skills Clinic"
- **Progress narratives** — "Passing improved 60% over 3 evaluations"
- **Natural language scoring** — speak scores, AI parses them

### 📊 Visual Analytics
- **Radar/spider charts** — instant visual skill profile for each player
- **Player comparison** — side-by-side view of 2-4 players
- **Progress tracking** — score trends across seasons
- **Score distributions** — see how the group performed overall
- **Evaluator calibration** — detect scoring bias between coaches

### 📄 Report Cards
- Professional, branded report cards
- Visual skill charts + AI narrative
- Player photo, rankings, benchmarks
- Self-assessment comparison (player vs coach)
- **PDF download** or shareable link
- Email directly to parents

### 🏆 Team Drafting
- **AI auto-balance** — equally strong teams in seconds
- Manual draft mode with live player rankings
- Position-based balancing (optional)
- **AI team analysis** — "Team Blue is weak in defense — consider swapping..."
- Export teams as CSV

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Portal
- Parents enter their email to see all their children's reports
- View scores, rankings, AI summaries
- Track progress across evaluations
- No account creation needed

### 🎯 Smart Evaluation Design
- Pre-built templates for **soccer, basketball, baseball** (more coming)
- Fully customizable skills, categories, and scoring scales
- Weighted scoring — some skills matter more than others
- Position-specific overrides (goalkeeper vs field player)
- Rubric descriptions — evaluators know what a "3" means
- Self-assessment — players rate themselves

### 🔧 Admin Dashboard
- Manage organizations, templates, players, events
- Import rosters via CSV
- View all conversations, reports, analytics
- QR code player check-in at events
- Season and year grouping
- Full data export (CSV/JSON)

### 🔗 Platform Integration
- Full REST API (44+ endpoints)
- Webhook notifications (event completed, reports sent)
- API authentication tokens
- White-label branding per organization
- Multi-sport support per organization

### 🧠 AI Coach Assistant
Ask the AI questions about your evaluation data:
- *"Which U12 players are ready for Travel?"*
- *"Who improved the most this season?"*
- *"What positions are we weakest at?"*
- *"Compare Marcus and Sofia's development trajectories"*
