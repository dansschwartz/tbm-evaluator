AI That Actually Helps

Every module has AI built in — not bolted on as an afterthought. Here's what AI does across the platform:

AI Player Reports

After evaluations, AI generates personalized narratives for each player: "Marcus shows exceptional ball control for a midfielder. His passing accuracy (4.5/5) is his standout skill. Focus on stamina training to match the demands of the position."

AI Development Plans

Custom 6-week training plans with position-specific drills, weekly schedules, and gap analysis. "As a Forward, Sofia should prioritize: Finishing circuits (3x/week), Sprint starts (3x/week), Blind-side runs (2x/week)."

AI Team Formation

Auto-build balanced teams from evaluation data. Considers skill scores, positions, and constraints. "Team Blue avg: 3.8. Team Red avg: 3.7. Teams balanced within 0.1 points."

AI Club Health Score

A single 0-100 score tells you how your club is performing across 7 dimensions: retention, coach ratios, financial aid, gender equity, fill rates, player development, satisfaction.

AI Email Drafting

Tell the AI "draft a weather cancellation for U12 parents" and get a professional, ready-to-send email in seconds — with the right tone, details, and next steps.

AI Operations Assistant

Ask questions in plain English: "Which teams don't have coaches?" "What fields are open Saturday?" "How many U10 players registered?" — instant answers from your data.

AI Schedule Generation

Input teams, fields, and constraints. AI generates an optimal game schedule — balanced home/away, no double-bookings, no team playing twice in one day.

AI Club Advisor

Proactive alerts: "Registration is down 12% vs last spring." "3 teams still need coaches." "Mo Gueye's SafeSport certification expires in 15 days."

AI Position Profiling

Analyzes each player against ideal position profiles. "Marcus is 82% Midfielder fit, 71% Forward fit." Helps coaches place players where they'll thrive.

AI Board Reports

Generate board-ready seasonal reports with executive summary, key metrics, year-over-year trends, and strategic recommendations — in one click.

AI Retention Predictor

Parent engagement scoring identifies families at risk of leaving before they go. "The Martinez family: 3 missed practices, no email opens in 60 days — flag for outreach."

AI Voice-to-Score

Coaches speak naturally: "Marcus — dribbling 4, passing strong, maybe 4.5, needs work on heading." AI parses into structured evaluation scores.
