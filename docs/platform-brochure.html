25+ AI Features — What No Competitor Offers

Every module has AI built in. Not bolted on. Not a chatbot gimmick. Real AI that generates reports, balances teams, predicts trends, builds training programs, analyzes video, optimizes fields, and automates workflows.

AI Player Report Cards

Personalized narratives using player name, scores, and position: "Marcus shows exceptional ball control for a midfielder. Focus on stamina to reach Travel level."

AI Development Plans

Custom 6-week training plans with position-specific drills, weekly schedules, and gap analysis. 30+ drill library for GK/DEF/MID/FWD.

AI Team Formation

Auto-balance teams from evaluation data. Position-aware, constraint-based. "Team Blue avg: 3.8. Team Red avg: 3.7. Balanced."

AI Club Health Score

0-100 composite across 7 dimensions: retention, coach ratios, financial aid, gender equity, fill rates, development, satisfaction.

AI Email Drafting

"Draft a weather cancellation for U12 parents" → professional email in seconds with the right tone, details, and next steps.

AI Operations Assistant

Ask in plain English: "Which teams don't have coaches?" "What fields are open Saturday?" Instant answers from your data.

AI Board Reports

Generate board-ready seasonal reports with executive summary, key metrics, year-over-year trends, and recommendations — one click.

AI Retention Predictor

Parent engagement scoring identifies families at risk of leaving. "Martinez family: 3 missed practices, flag for outreach."

AI Position Profiling

Analyzes each player against ideal position profiles. "Marcus is 82% Midfielder fit, 71% Forward fit."

AI Voice-to-Score

Coaches speak naturally: "Marcus — dribbling 4, passing strong." AI parses into structured evaluation scores automatically.

AI Schedule Generation

Input teams, fields, constraints. AI generates optimal game schedule — balanced home/away, no double-bookings, no conflicts.

AI Chatbot (Website)

24/7 AI assistant on the club website. Answers registration, schedule, cost, policy questions. Handles 80% of parent inquiries.

AI Training Programs

AI Video Analysis

AI Field Optimizer

AI Automation

When evaluation complete: auto-generate reports, email parents, assign training programs. Configurable if/then pipelines.
