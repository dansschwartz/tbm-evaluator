# TBM Platform — Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title
**Tiny But Mighty**
AI-Native Platform for Youth Sports Organizations
*"Run your entire club with AI at the core."*

## Slide 2: The Problem
- 7,500+ youth sports clubs in the US spend $3,000-$10,000+/year on disconnected tools
- PlayMetrics for registration, SkillShark for evaluations, spreadsheets for teams, email for communications
- No AI. No intelligence. No benchmarking.
- Club directors spend more time on admin than on their mission

## Slide 3: The Solution
**TBM Operations** — One AI-native platform that replaces everything
- 100+ features across 12 modules
- 20+ AI capabilities no competitor offers
- Benchmarked against 2,500+ clubs (IYSL framework)
- ~$20/month flat — no per-player, per-seat, or per-feature fees

## Slide 4: Product Demo
- Live demo: https://tbm-evaluator-production.up.railway.app
- Show: Evaluation → AI Report → Development Plan → Team Draft → Health Score

## Slide 5: The 12 Modules
1. Player Evaluation (AI reports, development plans)
2. Team Building (AI-balanced, position-aware)
3. Field Management (booking, optimization)
4. Scheduling (AI-generated, conflict detection)
5. Communications (AI email drafting)
6. Coach Management (certifications, matching)
7. Club Intelligence (health score, IYSL benchmarks)
8. Competition (league standings, stats)
9. Analytics (trends, retention, demographics)
10. Compliance (SafeSport, background checks)
11. Attendance (check-in, at-risk detection)
12. Document Vault (waivers, medical forms)
