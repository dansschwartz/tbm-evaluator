Squarespace Integration

Adding the chatbot to any Squarespace website takes 60 seconds:

1

Log into Squarespace → Settings → Advanced → Code Injection

2

Paste these two lines in the Footer section:

<!-- TBM AI Chatbot --> <script src="https://tbm-chatbot-production.up.railway.app/widget/chat-widget.js" data-org="dcsc"></script> <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://tbm-chatbot-production.up.railway.app/widget/chat-widget.css">
3

Click Save. The chat bubble appears immediately on every page.

Works on Squarespace, WordPress, Wix, Webflow — any website. Just two lines of code.
